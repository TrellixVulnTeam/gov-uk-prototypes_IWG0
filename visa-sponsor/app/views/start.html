Use this service to confirm that you will sponsor a person applying to settle in the UK. Also known as the "Sponsorship undertaking: form SU07".

This is to confirm that you will be responsible for the applicant’s maintenance, accommodation and care, without relying on public funds for at least 5 years, if they are applying to settle.

You can’t apply for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

Takes around 10 to 15 minutes and you'll need:

  • your National Insurance(NI) number
  • your employment details such as company name and full address with postcode
  • an email address for each person signing the application
  • one or more documents (opens in a section tab) from the list
{% endset -%} {% set documentsRequired %}

Note: The sponsor should provide evidence that he/she lives at the address given above. This evidence should include one or more documents from the list below.

  • Certified copy of deeds of sponsor's house
  • Letter from building society/bank confirming they hold a mortgage for the sponsor
  • Housing association/council rent book or other council letter confirming address of sponsor
  • Bill from a utility company (gas, water etc.)
  • Local authority letter (council tax)
  • Letter from any government department

Please note that applicants must also provide evidence of their sponsor's ability to maintain and accommodate them without recourse to public funds.
